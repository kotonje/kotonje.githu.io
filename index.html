About Kotonje Mixed

Our History

Kotenje Mixed Secondary School started in the year 2010 with 38 students, the number gradually increases ie 2014 we had 96 students, 2015 we had 139 students. Our KCSE enrollment has also been gradually increasing ie 2015 we had 61 candidates,2016 we had 54 candidates, 2017 we had 56 candidate and this year 2018 we have 72 candidates. The number of the TSC employered teachers has been increasing from 1 to 5 teachers since 2010, however through the BOG/PTA, Kotonje mixed sec has 13 teaching staff who are well trained and qualified to offer the relavant services. Kotonje Mixed is also priveleged to have other staffs who are the cooks, the guard and cleaners..
